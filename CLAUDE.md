# CLAUDE.md

This file provides guidance to Claude Code (claude.ai/code) when working with code in this repository.

## Project Overview

Memorial website dedicated to preserving the memory of a person — their personality, art, and story. The sister was killed during Russian aggression against Ukraine. The site centers on her drawings as the primary medium of remembrance.

Core principle: the visitor should feel not just loss, but presence — seeing through her eyes, feeling through her hands.

## Site Structure (from proposal)

- **Home**: portrait photo or best drawing, name, years of life, short dedication phrase
- **Her Story**: brief sincere narrative — who she was, how she died, no excess detail
- **Drawing Gallery**: all drawings with captions, zoom capability, optional memories per image
- **Words of Memory**: remembrances from family and friends (static text or guestbook)
- **Contact / Support** (optional)

## Design Guidelines

- Light, quiet background (white or soft gray)
- Minimal text on screen — let drawings speak
- Typography: light, readable (Georgia, Lora, or similar serif)
- Color palette derived from her drawings where possible
- No ads, no distracting elements

## Technical Notes

No codebase exists yet. The proposal suggests GitHub Pages as one platform option. When building:

- Static site preferred (no backend needed for initial version)
- All content in Ukrainian
- Images should be high-quality scans/photos of original drawings
- Site must work well on mobile devices
